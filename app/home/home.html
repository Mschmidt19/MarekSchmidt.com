Marek Schmidt

Hello, my name is Marek Schmidt. I am a 21 year old Front End Web Developer and Graphic Designer living in Irvine, California. With a background in Computer Engineering from the University of California, Santa Barbara, as well as self-practice with the MEAN stack, I was finally able to pinpoint the area I am most passionate about - design. Combining my love for development with my love for UI/UX and design in the form of front end web development has resonated perfectly with my adorations. Since finding my true calling, my only goal has been to enhance my skills and become a master of my craft. This site is meant to both document and showcase myself, my improvements, and the projects that have guided me through the journey.
